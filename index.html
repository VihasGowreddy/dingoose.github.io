My Interests

The three general areas of computer science that I am interested in are cyber security, software development, and machine learning.

In the sect of cyber security, I am most interested in the potential of blockchain as the idea of decentralized data is very interesting. Any faulty information that is on one device can be immediately cross checked with the set of data on every other device in the system making altering of data without getting caught very hard to achieve. Although cross checking with every other device in the system will lead to a bad time complexity, I believe that making algorithms using different graph models will be key to improving the worst-case time complexity.

The two areas of software development I am most interested in are application and systems development not only because I specialize with the languages used for these but also because applications are directly linked with the quality of life for customers these days.

Most animals learn through the ideas of classical and operant conditioning. Because this is associated with living beings, I am most fascinated with reinforcement learning in machine learning as it is the closest to conditioning because of the trial and error setup that is used to teach the machines the optimal solution.

Outside of computer science, I am a devout watcher and player of basketball as well as football.
