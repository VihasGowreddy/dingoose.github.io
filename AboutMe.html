About Me


The Person is More Than the Worker

Hi! My name is Vihas Gowreddy and I am currently a junior at the University of Texas at Dallas majoring in computer science. I am hoping to get an internship working on a project that has real influence on the company and the customers they serve to get the fulfillment of creating something that can better the lives of others.

Over the years of taking various courses in school and self-teaching, I have learned Java, C++, Python, HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap to an extent that I feel comfortable using them in the workforce. The language I have the most experience with is Java, which I have used to create both games and objective completing programs and I have used Processing 3, JCreator, and Eclipse integrated development environments to run my Java code.

The three general areas I want to be involved in are cyber security, software development, and, eventually, machine learning to gain experience in my three major interests before diving deeper into one as a career. I was first introduced to programming in my freshman year of high school when I took a web design course, but I only truly understood the practical usefulness of coding once I started taking a Java course in my junior year of high school.

With technology rapidly improving, I want to be a part of this historical time by contributing in a big way to this technological revolution.
